Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-17

This Privacy Policy explains how Vendora ("we", "us") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use the platform.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Email, display name, (optional) Telegram username, profile details.
  • Operational Data: Rates, orders, transactions, messages, push subscription tokens.
  • Technical Data: IP address (transient), user agent, timestamps, basic request logs.
  • Optional Integrations: Telegram chat IDs / usernames when you connect the bot.

2. How We Use Data

  • Authenticate and secure accounts
  • Process and display vendor operations (orders, rates, queries)
  • Send optional notifications (web push / Telegram)
  • Detect abuse or service misuse (rate limiting, anomaly review)
  • Improve reliability (aggregated, non‑personal metrics)

3. Legal Basis (where applicable)

We process data for legitimate interests in providing the Service, fulfilling your requests, and maintaining security. Where required, certain optional notifications rely on consent.

4. Cookies & Storage

The app uses local storage / session storage for authentication tokens and UI preferences. No third‑party ad trackers are embedded.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited data may be shared with infrastructure providers (hosting, email, error monitoring) strictly to operate the Service under confidentiality obligations.

6. Data Retention

Operational records (orders, transactions) are retained while your account is active. Logs may be rotated periodically. You may request deletion of your account which will remove personal profile data; certain aggregated or anonymized records may be kept for integrity and auditing.

7. Security

Transport encryption (HTTPS), JWT auth, per‑user throttling, and structured logging help protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; report concerns to security@vendora.page.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located in other jurisdictions. We apply reasonable safeguards consistent with this Policy.

9. Your Choices

  • Access or update profile data in the dashboard.
  • Disable web push or disconnect Telegram at any time.
  • Request account deletion via privacy@vendora.page.

10. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be communicated via in‑app notice or email where feasible. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@vendora.page
